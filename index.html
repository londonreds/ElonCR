Welcome

Our purpose: to make known & promote the values of the Republican Party. To recruit members to the club & Republican Party. To aid in the election of Republican candidates at all levels of government. To develop political leadership abilities among conservative students as preparation for future service to the party and their communities. We aim to create a space for political discussion on campus and where conservative students can share their beliefs and views with others who share their perspective.


Join us at a meeting or join our mailing list for more info.
